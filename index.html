Questions

The things you're wondering.

Why $1? What's the catch?
There is no catch. $1 covers compute, storage, and the permanent archival commitment. We're building the largest human monument ever created — not a subscription business. One payment, forever.
Is the Lifeprint real AI analysis or a template?
Every Lifeprint is generated in real-time by a frontier AI model analyzing your specific combination of inputs. Two people with the same age and country but different worries and dreams receive completely different results. It's not a template, not a horoscope — it's pattern recognition at scale.
Is my data stored or shared?
Your monument cell (name, photo, inscription) is stored in the archive — that's the point. Your Lifeprint answers are processed once by the AI and never stored. No accounts, no tracking, no selling of data. The AI forgets you the moment your analysis is generated.
What languages does it support?
16 languages including English, Spanish, Chinese, Arabic, Hindi, Russian, and more. Your inscription can be in any language. The Lifeprint is generated natively in your chosen language — not translated.
Will my cell really go to the Arctic / space?
Phase I is live now — your cell is digitally archived from the moment you claim it. Phases II and III activate at 50M and 100M entries respectively. The roadmap is public and the commitment is real. Every dollar raised is a step toward permanent preservation.
Can I update my cell later?
Your original cell is immutable — that's what makes it a monument. But you can claim additional cells as your life evolves. Many people return months or years later to add a new chapter to their archive.
